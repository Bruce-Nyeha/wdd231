Accra Chamber of Commerce

Site Plan WDD 231 Project

Site Name

Accra Metropolitan Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

This website serves as a central hub for businesses in Accra and surrounding areas. It provides resources for networking, event listings, business directories, training opportunities, and promotion of local economic growth. The site encourages collaboration among businesses, supports new entrepreneurs, and boosts visibility for members while promoting Accra as a thriving commercial center in Ghana.

Target Market

  • Small and medium business owners in Accra looking for growth tools and partnerships
  • Large corporate executives seeking networking and economic development insights
  • New residents and investors exploring business opportunities in the capital

Site Goals

  • Increase member engagement through events, directories, and useful resources
  • Attract new businesses to join the chamber and contribute to local economy
  • Position the chamber as the go-to authority for business support in Accra
  • Promote local shopping and services to strengthen community ties

User Personas

  1. Maria – Small Boutique Owner
    Age 34, owns a fashion shop in Osu. Wants to find affordable marketing support, join networking events, and list her business in the directory to attract more customers.
  2. Diego – Corporate Logistics Manager
    Age 45, works for a major shipping company. Interested in partnerships, policy updates, and connecting with other business leaders to expand operations in Accra.
  3. Francisco – New Resident & Entrepreneur
    Age 28, recently relocated to Accra. Needs information on local regulations, business registration, and community events to start his tech service company.

Scenarios

  • Maria visits the site to check membership fees, benefits, and how to register her boutique for the business directory.
  • Diego browses upcoming chamber events and workshops to network with potential partners in logistics and trade.
  • Francisco explores the site to find information on starting a business in Accra, including registration guides and local service providers.

SEO Plan

  • Include keywords such as "Accra Chamber of Commerce", "Accra business directory", "Ghana networking events", "Accra small business support" in titles, headings, and content
  • Create and verify a Google Business Profile for the chamber to appear in local searches
  • Encourage member businesses to add backlinks to the chamber site from their own websites
  • Add Google Analytics tracking code to monitor traffic and search performance

Design Brief

  • Primary Color: #006B3F (Ghana Green)
  • Secondary Color: #FCD116 (Ghana Gold)
  • Accent Color: #CE1126 (Ghana Red)
  • Background Color: #FFFFFF (White)
  • Text Color: #000000 (Black)
  • Font Family: 'Inter' (body) and 'Orbitron' (headings) from Google Fonts

Site Map (Simple List)

  • Home
  • About
  • Events & Calendar
  • Business Directory
  • Membership / Join
  • Contact

Wireframes (Description)

Desktop: Header with logo & name at top, horizontal navigation below, hero section with welcome message and call-to-action button, three-column layout for featured sections (events, directory, membership), footer at bottom.

Mobile: Header with logo & hamburger menu icon, collapsible navigation on click, single-column stacking for all content, larger touch-friendly buttons.
