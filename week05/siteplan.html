Site Plan – Accra Solar Gadget Guide

Individual Project – WDD 231 Web Frontend Development

1. Site Name

Accra Solar Gadget Guide

Reason: The name clearly communicates the focus: solar-powered gadgets relevant to people in Accra and Ghana facing power challenges. It is specific, memorable, searchable, and positions the site as a helpful resource rather than a sales-only shop.

Optional domain: accrasolarguide.com (available as of 2026 check)

2. Site Purpose

The site serves as a helpful guide for people in Ghana (especially Accra) interested in solar gadgets to save on electricity costs, reduce reliance on the grid, and live more sustainably. It provides:

  • Information about popular solar products (chargers, lights, fans, batteries)
  • Price comparisons and key features
  • Buying tips and recommendations
  • A contact/quote form for personalized advice

Overall goal: Educate users, build trust, and drive inquiries for purchases or consultations.

3. Scenarios

These represent typical questions from the target audience (small business owners, households, young professionals in Ghana):

  • What is the best affordable solar charger for my phone and small devices during load shedding?
  • Which solar fan gives the most power for the price under GH₵500?
  • How do I know if a solar battery is good quality and worth the investment?
  • Can I get help choosing the right solar setup for my small shop in Accra?

5. Typography

Two fonts for clean, modern look:

  • Headings: Orbitron (Google Fonts) – bold, futuristic, tech-energy vibe
    Usage: H1–H3, main titles
  • Body & Paragraphs: Inter (Google Fonts) – highly readable, neutral, professional
    Usage: All body text, descriptions, navigation
